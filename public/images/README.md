# Image Direction

Use warm, natural-light Myanmar cultural and travel scenes with an editorial guidebook feeling.

Good future replacements include places, food, crafts, rituals, streets, water, temples, markets, and quiet everyday details. Images can include people when respectful, unposed, and context-rich. Avoid random stock imagery, overly saturated edits, generic tourism poses, heavy filters, and anything that feels like a booking platform or commercial travel ad.

The current repeated image is the default visual fallback. Replace specific guide images over time while keeping the calm, cultural, paper-warm mood consistent.
